About

Founded in November 15, 2004, we started as web specialists focusing on small business consulting; later entered into software development, and training. Rayaguru focuses on delivering high quality business solutions to its clients through innovative technology solutions. We help clients to maximize the value of IT. We deliver a full range of complementary services and industry-specific, tailor-made solutions.

Our endeavor has always been to get an insight into the core competencies of our clients' businesses. We view technology as a prime mover in client's growth path. Thus we are able to strengthen the competitive edge in our clients' core products.

Rayaguru promptly responds to ever changing customer needs whether it's project execution or it's complete project outsourcing. We use structured methodologies and proven techniques in each phase of software development.
